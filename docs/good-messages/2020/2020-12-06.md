Assembled Bug Fixes

Metal Refinery:
Alternative string for Sappy now correctly displays on Metal Refineries when it should.
Fixed issue where Sheet Metal could appear as a resource from getting a Metal Refinery before any Foundries were built.

Vacuum Collapse:
Vacuum Collapse no longer removes a players Corrupted bonus entirely.
Dark Energy gained from Vacuum Collapse now properly contributes to the Dark Energy Earned stat. Current Dark Energy Earned is updated to account for any prior misalignment.

Wiki:
Both versions of Urban Planning no longer appear on the wiki, only the one that is currently applicable.
Electricity tech on the wiki now mentions that it unlocks the Power Grid tab.
Advanced Gun Emplacement tech on the wiki now mentions that it increases the effectiveness of Gate Turrets as well.
Wiki explanations of how to get The Misery, Crossed the Finish Line, and Garbage Pie now all mention that Demonic Infusion resets work for earning them.
Fixed a typo and incorrect value in a couple wiki strings.

Queue:
Queued ARPA projects now follow the same convention of designating "action" as the category it belongs to and "type" as the specific item being queued. Currently queued ARPA projects get their "action" and "type" corrected.
Queued Sheds/Barns will now update to their Barn/Warehouse versions when the appropriate research that changes their name is purchased. Same with queued Automated/Laser Turrets updating to Laser/Plasma Turrets.
Queued buildings and researches will now update their names upon switching languages.

Misc:
Evil races now have the Lumber Ritual hidden, as it does nothing for them. Currently assigned Lumber Rituals with Evil races get unassigned.
Rituals now tell now say how much they're boosting something by in their tooltips.
Plasmid, Anti-Plasmid, and Phage tooltips no longer incorrectly include the storage bonus from Harmonic Energy in their calclulation.
Fixed issue with Shrines not applying to Adamantite from Armed Mining Ships and displaying a much larger bonus than they were providing in the Adamantite tooltip.
Mega Factories now update the Industry page upon building them.
Fixed issue with Containers not appearing in resource Crate/Container modals after unlocking them from non-Container Port buildings.
Updated Pig Latin strings.

FvwmEvent: comply better with the GPL

Although fvwm has been under the GPL for some time, certain part of fvwm
(especially around modules) which have had different contriutions from
different authors over the years, have meant that certain copyright
messages have remained.

These copyright messages which are subject to modification as long as
said message remains intact, goes against the spirit of the GPL.
Indeed, in speaking with the authors in question, such practises were
common before the GPL was established as a means of licensing/modifiying
software.

The following authors were contacted and their responses indicate
they're happy for FvwmEvent's copyright notices to change:

* Mark Scott (2020-11-23):

	Thomas,

	Wow, itâ€™s great to hear from someone keeping that old code alive!  I
	honestly had forgotten about ever even writing that code. ðŸ˜Š  You are
	sure welcome to do whatever you like with that code base.

	I hereby release all copyright claims to any FvwmSound or FvwmAudio
	related code. Feel free to re-license it under GPL or any other license
	you feel appropriate.

* Mark Boyns (2020-11-23):

	Hi Thomas, you found the correct Mark. You have my permission to update
	the license as needed. Good luck with fvwm and your open source
	projects.

This change therefore removes the explicit copyright notices regarding
the behaviour of the copyright notices, but still firmly records their
contributions to FvwmSound/FvwmEvent.

The man page has been updated to reflect this as well.
